Her Life

Marietta Parisi was born and adopted from Guatemala.

She was raised on Long Island along with her three siblings.

(Left to Right: Her Sister Valentina, Her Brother Joe and Her Brother Christian.)

She attended schools within the East Meadow School District, and graduated in May of 2015 from East Meadow High School.

(Marietta attending her High School Graduation)

Since August of 2015 she has attended the University of New Haven persuing a Bachelor's of Science in Foresnic Science with a Concentration in Biology.

While studying at New Haven, Marietta recieved an extraordinary opportunity to Study Abroad to the University's Tuscany Campus for a six-week summer program.

While attending her classes she got the opportunity to participate in a mock crime scene in one of the local piazzas with her classmates.

(The Forensic Science students participating in their mock crime scenes.)

She expects to graduate with her Bachelor's of Science in May of 2019.

Her Extracurriculars

She has been a member of the University of New Haven Chargers Marching Band since her first year in the Fall of 2015, and has continued every year since.

Fall 2018 was her last season as a member, and she will be forever greatful for the different opportunities the Band gave her.

(The entire Marching Band before the Senior Performance)

She has also been a member of the University of New Haven's Winter Guard since her second year in the Spring of 2016, and has continued since. Spring 2019 will be her last season performing.

(The undefeated Independent A Class 2018 Champions)

She also became a Brother of the co-educational, Nu Omicron Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi, National Honorary Band Fraternity in the Fall of 2016 and is currently their Vice President of Projects.
