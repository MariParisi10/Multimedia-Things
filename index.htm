My School

The University of New Haven is the home of the Chargers.

The university consists of four main colleges on the main West Haven campus.

The colleges consists of Arts & Sciences, Business, Criminal Justice & Forensic Science and Engineering.

The University of New Haven has a total of 6,984 students, a majority of which are full time (5,901).

The student body is nearly a 50-50 split between males and females, with 25 percent of students identifying themselves as a member of an underrepresented group, and 306 with military veteran status.
